Office Administrator - Edinburgh

Company: TechBridge Solutions
Location: Edinburgh, UK (Hybrid - 3 days office, 2 days remote)
Salary: £28,000 - £32,000

About Us:
TechBridge Solutions is a growing technology consultancy based in Edinburgh, helping small and medium businesses with digital transformation. We're a friendly team of 15 passionate about making technology accessible.

The Role:
We're looking for an organised and proactive Office Administrator to keep our Edinburgh office running smoothly. You'll be the backbone of our operations, handling everything from financial admin to client communications.

Key Responsibilities:
- Manage day-to-day office operations and administrative tasks
- Handle bookkeeping, invoicing, and expense tracking using Xero
- Process monthly payroll and maintain employee records
- Coordinate with clients on project scheduling and communications
- Maintain and update company website content
- Manage Google Workspace environment and IT support for the team
- Prepare financial reports and budget summaries for management
- Support the leadership team with project documentation
- Organize team events and office supplies

Requirements:
- 2+ years of office administration or business management experience
- Experience with bookkeeping and financial administration
- Proficiency in Google Workspace and Microsoft Office
- Strong attention to detail and organisational skills
- Excellent written and verbal communication
- Experience with Xero or similar accounting software

Nice to Have:
- Experience in a technology company
- Website management skills
- Knowledge of digital tools and automation
- VAT returns experience
- Project coordination experience

We Value:
- Initiative and problem-solving
- Clear communication
- Reliability and attention to detail
- Positive, can-do attitude
- Willingness to learn new technologies
